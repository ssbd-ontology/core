Abstract

This module is a part of the [SSbD Core Ontology](https://w3id.org/ssbd). Matter is a physical entity that possess mass and occupies volume. This module provides a categorisation of different types of matter entities. At the top it categorise matter into molecular entities (discrete atoms or molecules) and different types of substances (homogeneous properties). A page illustrating showing how the SSbD Core Ontology categorise models can be found [here](https://ssbd-ontology.github.io/core/docs/matter.html). "
